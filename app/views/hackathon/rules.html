Rules

Disclaimer: Many of our rules are based off of the AngelHack Contest Rules

  1. Type of app: To compete in the hackathon you can build apps for Windows Phone 8, or Windows 8. However, tutorials will be focused upon Windows Phone 8.
  2. Fresh Code: You're app must be coded at the hackathon to be eligible for prizes. You can use open source libraries, follow tutorials, bring mockups and anything else you need. However you must code the actual app at the hackathon. We won't be actively policing this during the event, this rule is based off of a trust system.
  3. Ownership and IP: Teams have full ownership of everything they build at the event.
  4. Team size: Teams may not have more than 4 members.
  5. Presentations: If your app is selected as a finalist, you will present in front of everyone & our panel of judges during our final ceremonies.
  6. Judging & Winning: Everyone's apps will be judged upon their concept, design work as well as completeness.
    Each group's app will be judged by 2 judges initially to select finalists. After finalists have been chosen the full panel of judges will judge them during their presentations to determine final winners.

For more information about Hacker Academy please visit: www.hackeracademy.org
