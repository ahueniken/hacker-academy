About

Hacker Academy is proud to present our Windows Phone 8 hackathon. This hackathon is designed for students from any school , in any year, with any level of experience .

We're looking to provide a great experience for all levels of developers, if you're experienced you'll be able to compete for some of our fantastic prizes, and if you're a beginner this is a great opportunity to come out and learn!
