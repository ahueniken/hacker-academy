Frequently Asked Questions

Who can come?

Any university or high school student

Do I have to be an uber hacker whose built on windows phone before to come?

Nope, we will be running Introductory tutorials to Windows Phone App Development to help everyone along. However, if you are a rock-star hacker we want you to come too!

What should I bring with me to the event?

You should bring a power cord and laptop with you. Other than that it's up to you.

What meals are we served?

You will get lunch and supper on Saturday as well as lunch on Sunday.

Will there be wifi available there?

We will hopefully have wifi working for everyone on the day, however we cannot gaurantee it. Therefore you must be prepared to work without wifi.

More questions and answers will be posted soon.

For more information about Hacker Academy please visit: www.hackeracademy.org
